Introduction
This privacy policy ("Policy") explains how zoetic.xurlpay.org ("we," "us," or "our") 
collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our web3-based 
website (the "Site").

Information We Collect
We collect information about you in the following ways when you use the Site:

provide to us, such as your name and email address, when you create an account

device and usage of the Site through the use of cookies and other tracking 
technologies. This information may include your IP address, browser type, 
device type, and information about your interactions with the Site.

We use the information we collect about you for the following purposes:

To provide and maintain the Site: We use your information to operate and 
improve the Site and to provide the services and features offered through 
the Site.

To communicate with you: We may use your information to contact you with 
updates, newsletters, or other information about the Site or our products 
and services.

To personalize your experience: We may use your information to personalize 
your experience on the Site by showing you content and ads that are more 
relevant to your interests.

To analyze and improve the Site: We may use your information to understand 
how people use the Site and to identify and fix any issues.

Sharing Your Information
We may share your information with third parties in the following 
circumstances:

With service providers: We may share your information with service 
providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as hosting the Site 
or analyzing data.

With third-party advertisers: We may share your information with third-party 
advertisers to show you targeted ads on the Site or on other websites.

As required by law: We may disclose your information to third parties if 
we are required to do so by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court 
order.

Your Choices
You have the following choices regarding the collection and use of your 
information:

Opting out of targeted ads: You can opt out of targeted ads from third-party 
advertisers by adjusting the settings on your browser or device or by using 
opt-out tools provided by the Digital Advertising Alliance or the Network 
Advertising Initiative.

Disabling cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but 
this may affect your ability to use certain features of the Site.

Data Retention and Security
We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Site and 
fulfill the purposes described in this Policy. We take reasonable steps to 
protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure, but 
we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes 
to the Policy, we will post the updated Policy on this page and indicate the
